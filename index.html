Why RedPill?

RedPill (verb) is a process by which a person's perspective is radically transformed, introducing them to a new (and sometimes disturbing) understanding of the true nature of their situation.
RedPill (noun) is the TRUTH that radically transforms one’s perspective Our definition of Torah – GOD’s instruction, or teachings; the first 5 books of the Bible; context for understanding the rest of The Bible (This was the RedPill for us).



Who We Are

Founded in 2019, RedPill Torah is a ministry of teaching and mentoring. Our mission is to tell the world about the GOD of Israel, His eternal connection to Israel and how non-Israelites can be part of this eternal connection. The living GOD’s connection to Israel goes much deeper than citizenship; this connection is based on an eternal covenant. The GOD of Israel has given written guidance and instructions; we know them as The Bible. Too many believers in the GOD of Israel do not really understand His instructions. Many interpret them based on their own perspectives and context. The Bible was given in a Hebrew context, in (mostly) the Hebrew language and in an ancient Hebrew culture. If we don’t seek to understand The Bible from those perspectives, we can find ourselves following man-made religious practices in place of GOD’s instructions. You may have a deep love for the GOD of Israel, and a resolve to follow Him – we certainly hope so! Even your sincere, good intentions do not outweigh His instructions. All around the world, believers in the GOD of Israel are waking up to The Torah (first 5 books of The Bible). The Torah is the GOD of Israel’s context for understanding the rest of The Bible. Believers are realizing that Messiah Yeshua (Jesus) came to bring even more clarity to the GOD of Israel’s instructions. His Ruach HaKodesh (Holy Spirit) was sent to teach and guide us to the same instructions. The Bible is one book – not two, divided into “old” and “new” testaments! If you want to know the GOD of Israel’s instructions in the Hebrew context, join us on the journey! Follow us as we follow Messiah Yeshua!
